# Vocabulary Study Notes for Unit: Professional Communications

This section focuses on essential vocabulary and expressions relevant to professional communications in Italian, particularly in the context of business negotiations, job interviews, and contract agreements. Below is a comprehensive list of vocabulary items organized into thematic categories.

## 1. Greetings and Introductions

| Italian Word / Phrase | English Meaning            | Phonetic Pronunciation | Contextual Example Sentence                       | English Translation                               |
|-----------------------|----------------------------|------------------------|--------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------|
| Buongiorno            | Good morning               | bwon-jor-no            | Buongiorno, come posso aiutarLa?                 | Good morning, how can I assist you?              |
| Buonasera             | Good evening               | bwon-a-se-ra           | Buonasera, sono lieto di incontrarLa.            | Good evening, I am pleased to meet you.          |
| Piacere di conoscerLa| Nice to meet you           | pya-che-re dee ko-no-sher-la | Piacere di conoscerLa, sono Marco.               | Nice to meet you, I am Marco.                    |
| Come sta?             | How are you?              | koh-meh stah           | Come sta, signora Rossi?                          | How are you, Mrs. Rossi?                          |
