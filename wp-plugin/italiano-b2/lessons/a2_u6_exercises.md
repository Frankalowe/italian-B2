# Study Notes: Exercises for "Intro to Imperfect & Future Tenses"

## Overview
In this segment, we will focus on exercises that help reinforce the understanding and usage of the **Imperfetto** (Imperfect) and **Futuro Semplice** (Simple Future) tenses in Italian. These exercises will include fill-in-the-blank sentences, translation tasks, situational writing prompts, and a multiple-choice quiz.

---

## 1. Fill-in-the-Blank Practice Sentences

### Instructions
Complete the following sentences by filling in the blanks with the correct form of the verb in either the Imperfetto or Futuro Semplice tense.

1. Quando ero bambino, io _______ (giocare) a calcio ogni giorno.  
   *(Hint: Describe a past habit.)*

2. Domani, noi _______ (andare) al cinema con gli amici.  
   *(Hint: Future plan.)*

3. Mentre lei _______ (leggere), io _______ (ascoltare) la musica.  
   *(Hint: Two actions happening simultaneously in the past.)*

4. L'estate scorsa, il tempo _______ (essere) molto bello.  
   *(Hint: Describe past weather.)*

5. Tra un anno, io _______ (iniziare) un nuovo lavoro.  
   *(Hint: Future prediction.)*

## 2. Sentence-Translation Exercises

### Instructions
Translate the following sentences from English to Italian.

1. I used to play tennis every Saturday.
2. Tomorrow, we will go to the beach.
3. Last winter, it was very cold.
4. She will study for the exam next week.
5. When I was young, I liked to draw.

## 3. Situational Writing Prompts

### Instructions
Write a short paragraph in Italian based on the given prompts.

1. **You are at a café. Write a message ordering a coffee and a pastry. Include a future plan for the afternoon.**
   
2. **Describe your favorite hobby in the past and how it has changed over time. Use both Imperfetto and Futuro Semplice.**

3. **Imagine you are talking about the weather. Describe what the weather was like last summer and predict what it will be like next summer.**

---

## 4. Multiple Choice Grammar Quiz

### Instructions
Choose the correct answer for each question.

1. Which of the following is the correct Imperfetto form of "mangiare" for "io"?
   - A) mangiavo
   - B) mangio
   - C) mangerò

2. What is the correct Futuro Semplice form of "partire" for "noi"?
   - A) partivamo
   - B) partiremo
   - C) partiamo

3. Which sentence correctly uses the Imperfetto tense?
   - A) Ieri, io vado al mercato.
   - B) Quando ero piccolo, io andavo al parco.
   - C) Domani, noi andiamo al ristorante.

4. How do you say "I will play" in Italian?
   - A) Io giocavo
   - B) Io giocherò
   - C) Io gioco

5. Which of the following describes a past continuous action?
   - A) Mentre lui parla, io ascolto.
   - B) Ieri, lui parlava quando sono arrivato.
   - C) Domani, lui parlerà.

---

## 5. Answer Key and Explanations

### Fill-in-the-Blank Answers
1. giocavo
2. andremo
3. leggeva, ascoltavo
4. era
5. inizierò

### Sentence-Translation Answers
1. Giocavo a tennis ogni sabato.
2. Domani, andremo in spiaggia.
3. L'inverno scorso, faceva molto freddo.
4. Studierà per l'esame la prossima settimana.
5. Quando ero giovane, mi piaceva disegnare.

### Situational Writing Prompts
- Responses will vary; ensure correct use of Imperfetto and Futuro Semplice.

### Multiple Choice Answers
1. A) mangiavo
2. B) partiremo
3. B) Quando ero piccolo, io andavo al parco.
4. B) Io giocherò
5. B) Ieri, lui parlava quando sono arrivato.

### Grammatical Explanations
- **Imperfetto** is used for ongoing actions in the past, habitual actions, and descriptions.
- **Futuro Semplice** is used for actions that will happen in the future.
