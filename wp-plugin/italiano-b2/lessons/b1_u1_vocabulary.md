# Vocabulary Study Notes for Unit: "Imperfetto & Past Contrasts"

In this segment, we will focus on vocabulary related to school and university life, subjects, and childhood memories. This vocabulary will help you express past experiences and contrasts effectively while using the Imperfetto and Passato Prossimo tenses.
