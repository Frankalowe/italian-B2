# [ANSWERS] – Professor's Key and Explanations

### Section 1: Fill in the Blanks Answers

1.  Io vivo **a** cinque anni. (Incorrect structure for this context. *Correction:* Io vivo **a** Roma **da** cinque anni. *Explanation:* We use **a** for cities/addresses and **da** for duration of time.)
2.  Il treno parte **da** Milano. (*Explanation:* **Da** indicates the starting point or origin.)
3.  La lezione inizia **alle** nove. (Note: While the prompt only listed the word bank, for time, we use **alle** for specific hours. If forced to use only the bank words, the closest concept is **a** or **in**, but **alle** is the correct structure for time.)
4.  Viaggiamo **in** macchina. (*Explanation:* **In** is used for modes of transportation (in a car, in a train, in a plane).)
5.  Il mio compleanno è **a** maggio. (*Explanation:* We use **a** for months.)
6.  Parliamo **al** telefono. (Note: The correct preposition for phone calls is **al** (a + il). If strictly limited to the word bank, **con** (with) is sometimes used contextually, but **al** is standard.)
7.  L'estate è **tra** luglio e agosto. (*Explanation:* **Tra** is used to indicate something is located between two points.)
8.  Sono **alle** tre ore. (Note: We use **alle** for specific times. If forced to use only the word bank, **a** is used for general time, but **alle** is necessary for precision.)
9.  Lavoriamo **di** lunedì. (*Explanation:* **Di** is used to indicate the start/range of days or periods.)
10. Sono **a** casa. (*Explanation:* **A** is used for location/being at a place.)

### Section 2: Translation Answers

1.  It is ten o'clock in the morning.
    *   **Sono le dieci del mattino.** (or *Sono le dieci in mattinata.*)
2.  We travel by bus.
    *   **Viaggiamo in autobus.** (or *Viaggiamo in macchina.*)
3.  I am from Spain.
    *   **Sono dalla Spagna.** (or *Sono spagnolo/spagnola.*)
4.  The book is on the table.
    *   **Il libro è sul tavolo.** (or *Il libro è sopra il tavolo.*)
5.  I study in the library.
    *   **Studio in biblioteca.** (or *Studio alla biblioteca.*)

### Section 4: Multiple Choice Quiz Answers

1.  **B) Che ora sono?** (This is the standard way to ask for the time.)
2.  **B) con** (We use **con** to indicate the means or accompaniment.)
3.  **C) in** (We use **in** for larger areas or general locations, e.g., *in Italia*, *in casa*.)
4.  **B) mercoledì** (If today is Tuesday, tomorrow is Wednesday.)
5.  **A) Io sono in città.** (We use **essere** (to be) with **in** to describe location.)
