# Future Tense Mastery: Exercises

## 1. Fill-in-the-Blank Practice Sentences

Complete the following sentences by filling in the blanks with the correct form of the verb in the futuro semplice or futuro anteriore. 

1. Domani, io _______ (iniziare) un nuovo lavoro in un'azienda internazionale.  
   *(Translation Tip: "Tomorrow, I will start a new job in an international company.")*

2. Se tutto va bene, noi _______ (finire) il progetto entro la prossima settimana.  
   *(Translation Tip: "If all goes well, we will finish the project by next week.")*

3. Quando tu _______ (arrivare) a casa, io _______ (preparare) la cena.  
   *(Translation Tip: "When you arrive home, I will prepare dinner.")*

4. Entro la fine dell'anno, loro _______ (completare) la loro formazione.  
   *(Translation Tip: "By the end of the year, they will have completed their training.")*

5. Se avessi tempo, io _______ (studiare) di più per il mio esame.  
   *(Translation Tip: "If I had time, I would study more for my exam.")*

## 2. Sentence-Translation Exercises

4. We will work together on this project.

## 3. Situational Writing Prompts

Write a short paragraph in Italian based on the following prompts.

1. You are at a recruitment fair. Write a message to a potential employer expressing your interest in their company and what you hope to achieve in your career.

2. Imagine you are discussing your future plans with a friend. Write about what you will do in the next five years regarding your job and personal life.

3. You just received a job offer. Write a message to your family informing them about your new position and what you will do next.

## 4. Multiple Choice Grammar Quiz

Choose the correct answer for each question.

1. Quale di queste frasi è corretta?
   - A) Io mangio la pizza domani.
   - B) Io mangerò la pizza domani.
   - C) Io mangiavo la pizza domani.

2. Se tu _______ (studiare) di più, avresti superato l'esame.
   - A) studierai
   - B) studi
   - C) studieresti

3. Loro _______ (completare) il progetto entro venerdì.
   - A) completeranno
   - B) completano
   - C) completavano

4. Quando io _______ (arrivare) a casa, tu _______ (giocare) ai videogiochi.
   - A) arriverò / giocherai
   - B) arrivo / giochi
   - C) arrivavo / giocavi

5. Entro la fine dell'anno, noi _______ (avere) un nuovo ufficio.
   - A) avremo
   - B) abbiamo
   - C) avevamo

### Grammatical Explanations
- **Futuro Semplice**: Used to express actions that will happen in the future. Formed by adding specific endings to the infinitive form of the verb.
- **Futuro Anteriore**: Used to express actions that will have been completed before a certain point in the future. Formed with the future of "avere" or "essere" + past participle of the main verb.
- **Hypothetical Scenarios**: Often expressed using "se" (if) + conditional forms to indicate possibilities or assumptions about the future.
