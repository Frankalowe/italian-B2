# 📚 Masterclass Vocabulary Sheet: Modal Verbs & Expressing Needs (A2 Level)

**Unit Focus:** Modal Verbs (*potere, volere, dovere, sapere*) in the Past Tense + Health & Emergency Vocabulary.

This vocabulary set is designed to equip you with the necessary words to discuss your health, express your needs, and talk about past situations using modal verbs.

## Theme 3: Expressing Needs and Abilities (Esprimere Bisogni e Capacità)

This section focuses on the functional language required to use the modal verbs (*potere, volere, dovere, sapere*) in the context of expressing needs and past obligations.

| Italian Word / Phrase | English Meaning | Phonetic Pronunciation Guide | Contextual Example Sentence (Italian) | English Translation of Example Sentence |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Potere** (Past Tense) | Could / Was able to | poh-TEH-reh | Io potevo andare. | I could go. |
| **Volere** (Past Tense) | Wanted | voh-LEH-reh | Io volevo aiuto. | I wanted help. |
| **Dovere** (Past Tense) | Had to / Must | doh-VEH-reh | Io dovevo restare a casa. | I had to stay at home. |
| **Sapere** (Past Tense) | To know (how to) | sah-peh-reh | Io sapevo come fare. | I knew how to do it. |
| **Avere bisogno di** | To need | ah-veh-reh bee-ZOH-nyo dee | Io avevo bisogno di aiuto. | I needed help. |
| **Non poter** | Not to be able to | non poh-TEH-reh | Non potevo parlare. | I could not speak. |
| **Volere di** | To want to | voh-LEH-reh dee | Io volevo di uscire. | I wanted to go out. |
| **Dovere di** | To have to / Must | doh-VEH-reh dee | Io dovevo di studiare. | I had to study. |
| **Sapere come** | To know how | sah-peh-reh KOH-meh | Io sapevo come guarire. | I knew how to heal. |
| **Non sapere** | Not to know | non sah-peh-reh | Io non sapevo la risposta. | I did not know the answer. |
| **Potere fare** | To be able to do | poh-TEH-reh fah-reh | Non potevo fare nulla. | I could not do anything. |
| **Volere di fare** | To want to do | voh-LEH-reh dee fah-reh | Io volevo di partire. | I wanted to leave. |
| **Dovere fare** | To have to do | doh-VEH-reh fah-reh | Io dovevo fare la spesa. | I had to do the shopping. |

## Theme 4: Emergency Expressions (Espressioni di Emergenza)

These are crucial phrases for communicating urgent needs in a stressful situation.

| Italian Word / Phrase | English Meaning | Phonetic Pronunciation Guide | Contextual Example Sentence (Italian) | English Translation of Example Sentence |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Aiuto!** | Help! | EYE-oo-toh | Aiuto! Sono in pericolo! | Help! I am in danger! |
| **Chiamate la polizia** | Call the police | kee-ah-mah-teh lah poh-lee-TSEE-ah | Chiamate la polizia subito! | Call the police immediately! |
| **Chiamate un'ambulanza** | Call an ambulance | kee-ah-mah-teh oon-mboo-LAN-zah | Chiamate un'ambulanza! | Call an ambulance! |
| **Sono malissimo** | I am very sick | soh-no mah-LEES-see-moh | Sono malissimo, ho bisogno di aiuto. | I am very sick, I need help. |
| **È un'emergenza** | It is an emergency | eh oon-mer-jeh-EN-tsah | Questa è un'emergenza medica. | This is a medical emergency. |
| **Dove sono?** | Where are you? | doh-veh SOH-noh? | Dove sono? Sono in ospedale. | Where are you? I am in the hospital. |
| **Ho bisogno di** | I need | oh bee-ZOH-nyo dee | Ho bisogno di un medico. | I need a doctor. |
| **Per favore** | Please | pehr fah-VOH-reh | Per favore, aiutatemi. | Please, help me. |
