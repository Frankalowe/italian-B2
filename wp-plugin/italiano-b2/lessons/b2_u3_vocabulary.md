# Vocabulary Study Notes for Unit: "Hypothetical Periods (Periodo Ipotetico)"

In this section, we will explore vocabulary related to hypothetical situations, speculation, environmental sustainability, and climate solutions. This vocabulary will help you express hypothetical scenarios effectively while also enriching your understanding of relevant themes in Italian.
