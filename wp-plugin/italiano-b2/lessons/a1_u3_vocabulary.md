***

### 💡 Professor's Note on Grammar Application:

Notice how the verb changes based on the subject:

*   **Essere (To be):** Used for permanent states, identity, and descriptions. (e.g., *Io **sono** stanco* - I **am** tired).
*   **Avere (To have):** Used for possession, age, and many physical states. (e.g., *Io **ho** fame* - I **have** hunger).
*   **Regular Verbs:** The ending changes depending on the subject pronoun (*io, tu, lui/lei, noi, voi, loro*). For example, with *parlare*, the endings are: *parlo, parli, parla, parliamo, parlate, parlano*. This is the core structure you will master in the next steps!
