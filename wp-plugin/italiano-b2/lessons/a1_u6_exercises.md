# [ANSWERS]

### Part 1: Fill-in-the-Blanks Answers

1. **Io voglio** (or **Io prendo**) [I want / I take]
    *(Context: Expressing a desire or action.)*
2. **Noi mangiamo** [We eat]
    *(Context: Present tense conjugation for 'noi'.)*
3. **Hai** [You have]
    *(Context: Conjugation for 'tu'.)*
4. **Loro fanno** (or **Loro comprano**) [They do / They buy]
    *(Context: Present tense conjugation for 'loro'.)*
5. **Io voglio**, ma non **ho** [I want, but I do not have]
    *(Context: Combining desire with negation of possession.)*

### Part 2: Sentence Translation Answers

1. **Io compro pane e latte.** (or: *Compro pane e latte.*)
2. **Noi non mangiamo carne.**
3. **Vuoi acqua?**
4. **Lei non è a casa.** (or: *Non è a casa.*)
5. **Io ordino una pizza al bar.** (or: *Io ordino una pizza in un caffè.*)

### Part 3: Situational Writing Prompts (Example Answers)

*Non voglio zucchero.* (I do not want sugar.)

**Prompt 3 Example:**

### Part 4: Multiple Choice Quiz Answers

1. **b) sono** (The structure is *essere* - to be. *Io sono fame* is incorrect; the correct expression is *Io ho fame*.)
2. **a) mangia** (The subject is 'La mamma' (she), requiring the third-person singular conjugation.)
3. **b) vogliamo** (The subject is 'Noi' (we), requiring the correct conjugation of *volere*.)
4. **b) non sono** (The correct expression for 'I am not hungry' uses the verb *essere*.)
5. **a) Io non ho soldi.** (The correct structure for possession is *avere* + object.)
