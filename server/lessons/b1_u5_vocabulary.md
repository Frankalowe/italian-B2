# Unit: The Present Conditional Mood
## Subtopic: Vocabulary

In this segment, we will focus on vocabulary related to healthcare, booking medical appointments, communicating with a doctor, and describing symptoms. This vocabulary will help you express polite requests, advice, desires, and hypothetical situations in the context of healthcare.

This vocabulary will be essential for expressing yourself in various healthcare-related contexts. Practice using these words and phrases in sentences to enhance your understanding and fluency in Italian.
