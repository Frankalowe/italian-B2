**Exercises**
================

### Fill-in-the-Blank Practice Sentences

Test your understanding of definite and indefinite articles, noun endings, and noun-adjective agreement with these 5 sentences.

1. Il mio amico _______ (nome) è Giovanni.
[Translation: "The name of my friend is Giovanni."]
2. Ho visto un libro _______ (titolo) sulla tavola.
[Translation: "I saw a book titled '...' on the table."]
3. La mia sorella ha una casa _______ (colore) in campagna.
[Translation: "My sister has a house with a color in the countryside."]
4. Il mio gatto è molto grande e pesa _______ (pounds).
[Translation: "My cat is very big and weighs ... pounds."]
5. I miei genitori hanno comprato un regalo per la nostra amica _______ (nome) che si chiama Sofia.
[Translation: "My parents bought a gift for our friend named Sofia."]

### Sentence-Translation Exercises

Translate the following sentences from English to Italian.

1. My name is John and I'm 25 years old.
2. The book on the table has a red cover.
3. My sister's house in the countryside has a beautiful garden.
4. The cat is sleeping on my lap.
5. We're going to the beach tomorrow with our friends.

### Situational Writing Prompts

Write a short message or paragraph using the following prompts:

1. You are at a cafe and want to order a coffee.
2. You see a friend wearing a new shirt and want to compliment them.
3. You are describing your favorite book to a classmate.
