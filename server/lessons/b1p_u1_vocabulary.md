# Vocabulary Study Notes: Present Subjunctive (Congiuntivo Presente)

In this section, we will focus on vocabulary related to social changes, civil rights, and community development. Understanding these terms will help you express opinions, emotions, and doubts, which are essential when using the present subjunctive in Italian.
