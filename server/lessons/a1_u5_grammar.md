**Grammar Study Notes**
=======================

### 1. Simple Prepositions

#### Definition

Simple prepositions are words that indicate the relationship between a noun or pronoun and other elements in a sentence.

#### List of Common Prepositions

| Preposition | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| di | of, from |
| a | to, at |
| da | from, since |
| in | in, on |
| con | with |
| su | on, above |
| per | for, by |
| tra | between, among |
| fra | between, among (informal) |

#### Usage Examples

* Io vado a scuola. (I go to school.)
* L'ho comprato di seconda mano. (I bought it second-hand.)
* Sono da Roma. (I'm from Rome.)
* Sono in Italia. (I am in Italy.)
* Vengo con mio fratello. (I come with my brother.)

#### Irregular Prepositions

| Preposition | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| tra | between, among |
| fra | between, among (informal) |

Note: Tra and fra are often used interchangeably, but fra is more informal.

### 6. Example Sentences

1. Io vado in Italia ogni anno. (I go to Italy every year.)
2. Sono nato a Roma il 12 gennaio 1990. (I was born in Rome on January 12th, 1990.)
3. Che ore sono? Sono le cinque e mezzo. (What time is it? It's half past five.)
4. Vengo con mio fratello. (I come with my brother.)
5. Io vado al cinema ogni venerdì sera. (I go to the movies every Friday night.)
6. Sono da Roma, ma vivo a Milano ora. (I'm from Rome, but I live in Milan now.)
7. L'ho comprato di seconda mano. (I bought it second-hand.)
8. Io vado al lavoro ogni lunedì mattina. (I go to work every Monday morning.)

Note: These example sentences are designed to illustrate the usage of prepositions, dates and time, and other grammar concepts in context.
