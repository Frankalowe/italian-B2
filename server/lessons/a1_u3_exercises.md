**Exercises**
================

### Fill-in-the-Blank Practice Sentences

Test your understanding of the present indicative tense of auxiliary verbs 'essere' and 'avere', as well as regular verbs ending in -are, -ere, and -ire.

1. **Io _______ studente di italiano.** [I am a Italian student.]
2. **La mia famiglia _______ a Roma.** [My family lives in Rome.]
3. **Il mio nome _______ Maria.** [My name is Maria.]
4. **Sono un _______ di inglese.** [I am an English teacher.]
5. **Ho una sorella che _______ 10 anni.** [I have a sister who is 10 years old.]

### Sentence-Translation Exercises

Translate the following sentences from English to Italian.

1. I am a student of Italian.
2. My family lives in Milan.
3. My name is Luca and I am 25 years old.
4. I am an English teacher.
5. She has two brothers who are 12 and 15 years old.

### Situational Writing Prompts

Write the following messages in Italian:

1. **You are at a cafe, write a message ordering a cappuccino.**
2. **You are introducing yourself to a friend's family, write a brief introduction.**
3. **You are writing a post on social media about your daily routine, write a short paragraph.**

### Multiple Choice Grammar Quiz

Choose the correct answer for each question.

1. What is the present indicative form of 'essere' in the first person singular?
a) Sono
b) Ero
c) Sarò
d) Fu

2. Which verb ending indicates that the verb is regular and ends in -are?
a) -are
b) -ere
c) -ire
d) -isc

3. What is the present indicative form of 'avere' in the third person plural?
a) Hanno
b) Ho
c) Avere
d) Abbia

4. Which sentence uses the correct present indicative form of a regular verb ending in -are?
a) Io mangio una mela.
b) Io mangia una mela.
c) Io mangi una mela.
d) Io mangè una mela.

5. What is the meaning of the phrase "Sono un _______ di inglese"?
a) I am a teacher of English
b) I am an English student
c) I am from England
d) I am an English speaker
