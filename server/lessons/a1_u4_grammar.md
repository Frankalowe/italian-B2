**Unit 1: Adjectives, Possessives & Demonstratives**
=============================================

### Subtopic: Grammar

#### 1.1 Adjective Agreement

In Italian, adjectives must agree with the noun they modify in terms of:

* Number (singular or plural)
* Gender (masculine or feminine)

**Adjective Agreement Table**

| Adjective | Singular Masculine | Singular Feminine | Plural |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Regular Adjectives | -o, -e, -i, -a | -a, -e, -i, -o | -i, -e, -i, -o |
| Irregular Adjectives | (see below) | (see below) | (see below) |

**Irregular Adjectives**

| Adjective | Singular Masculine | Singular Feminine | Plural |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| buono | buona | buone |
| cattivo | cattiva | cattivi |
| grande | grande | grandi |

#### 1.2 Possessive Adjectives

Possessive adjectives indicate ownership or possession.

**Possessive Adjective Table**

| Possessive Adjective | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| mio | my, mine (masculine) |
| mia | my, mine (feminine) |
| tuo | your (masculine and feminine) |
| tua | your (feminine) |
| nostro | our, ours (masculine and feminine) |
| nostra | our, ours (feminine) |
| vostro | your (formal, masculine and feminine) |
| vostra | your (formal, feminine) |
| loro | their, theirs |

**Possessive Adjective Agreement**

* Possessive adjectives agree with the noun they modify in terms of number and gender.

#### 1.3 Demonstrative Adjectives

Demonstrative adjectives indicate proximity or distance.

**Demonstrative Adjective Table**

| Demonstrative Adjective | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| questo | this (near speaker) |
| quello | that (far from speaker) |

**Demonstrative Adjective Agreement**

* Demonstrative adjectives agree with the noun they modify in terms of number and gender.

#### 1.4 Article Agreement

Articles must agree with the noun they modify in terms of:

* Number (singular or plural)
* Gender (masculine or feminine)

**Article Table**

| Article | Singular Masculine | Singular Feminine | Plural |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| il, lo | (masculine singular) | la | i, gli |
| una, un | (feminine singular) | le |

### Example Sentences

1. **Adjective Agreement**

Il libro è rosso. → The book is red.

La casa è grande. → The house is big.

2. **Possessive Adjectives**

Mio fratello è alto. → My brother is tall.

La mia sorella è giovane. → My sister is young.

3. **Demonstrative Adjectives**

Questo libro è interessante. → This book is interesting.

Quella casa è bella. → That house is beautiful.

4. **Article Agreement**

Il cane è giallo. → The dog is yellow.

Le ragazze sono felici. → The girls are happy.
