# Vocabulary Study Notes: Relative Pronouns in Italian

## Introduction
In this segment, we will focus on vocabulary related to housing search, renting apartments, and administrative processes. Understanding these terms will help you navigate conversations and written texts involving relative pronouns such as "che," "cui," "chi," and "quello che/ciò che."

|-----------------------|-----------------|------------------------|-----------------------------|---------------------|

| contratto             | contract        | con-trah-toh           | Ho firmato il contratto per l'appartamento. | I signed the contract for the apartment. |
| spese condominiali    | condominium fees | speh-zeh con-do-min-ee-ah-lee | Le spese condominiali sono incluse nel prezzo. | The condominium fees are included in the price. |

### 3. Expressions Related to Relative Pronouns

| Italian Word / Phrase | English Meaning | Phonetic Pronunciation | Contextual Example Sentence | English Translation |
|-----------------------|-----------------|------------------------|-----------------------------|---------------------|
| che                   | that/who        | keh                    | L'appartamento che ho trovato è perfetto. | The apartment that I found is perfect. |
| cui                   | whom/which      | kwee                   | Il proprietario cui ho parlato è disponibile. | The landlord whom I spoke to is available. |
| chi                   | who/whoever     | kee                    | Chi cerca trova. | He who seeks finds. |
| quello che            | what/that which | kweh-loh keh           | Quello che voglio è un appartamento spazioso. | What I want is a spacious apartment. |
| ciò che               | what            | choh keh               | Ciò che conta è la posizione. | What matters is the location. |

This vocabulary sheet provides essential terms and phrases related to housing, renting, and administrative processes, which are crucial for understanding and using relative pronouns in Italian. Practice using these words in sentences to enhance your fluency and comprehension.
