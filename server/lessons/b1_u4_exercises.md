# Unit: Relative Pronouns - Exercises

## 1. Fill-in-the-Blank Practice Sentences

Complete the following sentences with the appropriate relative pronoun: 'che', 'cui', 'chi', or 'quello che/ciò che'.

1. La casa _______ ho affittato è molto spaziosa.  
   (The house _______ I rented is very spacious.)

2. Gli studenti _______ studiano in biblioteca sono molto diligenti.  
   (The students _______ study in the library are very diligent.)

3. Il contratto _______ ho firmato è molto chiaro.  
   (The contract _______ I signed is very clear.)

4. Chi _______ cerca un appartamento deve essere paziente.  
   (He who _______ is looking for an apartment must be patient.)

5. Questo è il motivo per _______ ho scelto di vivere qui.  
   (This is the reason for _______ I chose to live here.)

## 2. Sentence-Translation Exercises

Translate the following sentences from English to Italian, using the appropriate relative pronouns.

1. The apartment that I found is very affordable.
   
2. The person who helps with the paperwork is very friendly.

3. The neighborhood where I live is very quiet.

4. I like the house that has a garden.

5. Whoever wants to visit can come this weekend.

## 3. Situational Writing Prompts

Write a short paragraph in Italian based on the following prompts. Use relative pronouns where appropriate.

1. You are looking for a new apartment. Describe the features you want and the reasons why you need to move.

2. Write a message to a friend about a person you met at a housing fair. Include what they do and how they can help you.

3. Describe a neighborhood you like, mentioning the places you enjoy visiting and the people you often see there.
