# Unit: Reading the Italian Press
## Subtopic: Vocabulary

In this segment, we will focus on enriching your vocabulary with terms and expressions commonly found in Italian newspapers, particularly in the context of political and economic reporting. This vocabulary will help you understand and analyze journalistic prose, including the use of syntactical structures like double negatives and passive voice.
