# Vocabulary Study Notes: Intro to Imperfect & Future Tenses

In this segment, we will focus on vocabulary related to hobbies, sports, weather descriptions, and simple predictions. This vocabulary will be essential for constructing sentences in both the Imperfetto (Imperfect) and Futuro Semplice (Simple Future) tenses. Below is a comprehensive list of words and phrases organized thematically.
