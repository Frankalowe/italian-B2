# Vocabulary Study Notes: The Gerund (Gerundio)

In this section, we will explore vocabulary related to economic terms, inflation, market trends, and finance, which will help you understand and use the gerund in various contexts. The vocabulary is organized thematically to facilitate learning.

|-----------------------|-----------------------|------------------------------|------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------|
| economia              | economy                | eh-koh-nee-mah              | L'economia italiana sta crescendo rapidamente.  | The Italian economy is growing rapidly.                  |
| inflazione            | inflation              | in-flah-tsee-oh-neh         | L'inflazione sta aumentando ogni anno.         | Inflation is increasing every year.                       |
| mercato               | market                 | mehr-kah-toh                | Il mercato azionario è molto volatile.         | The stock market is very volatile.                        |
| investimento          | investment             | in-vest-ee-men-toh          | L'investimento in tecnologia è fondamentale.   | Investment in technology is essential.                    |
| risparmio             | savings                | rees-par-mee-oh             | Il risparmio è importante per il futuro.       | Savings are important for the future.                     |
| debito                | debt                   | deh-bee-toh                 | Il debito pubblico è in aumento.               | Public debt is on the rise.                               |
| tasso d'interesse     | interest rate          | tah-soh deen-teh-reh-seh    | Il tasso d'interesse è molto basso.           | The interest rate is very low.                            |
| capitale              | capital                | kah-pee-tah-leh             | Il capitale è necessario per avviare un'impresa.| Capital is necessary to start a business.                |

## Expressions Using the Gerund

| Italian Expression     | English Meaning       | Phonetic Pronunciation Guide | Contextual Example Sentence                     | English Translation of the Example Sentence               |
|-----------------------|-----------------------|------------------------------|------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------|
| pur sapendo           | even knowing           | poor sah-pehn-doh            | Pur sapendo dei rischi, ha investito comunque. | Even knowing the risks, he invested anyway.              |

| facendo attenzione     | paying attention       | fah-chen-doh ah-ten-zee-oh-neh | Facendo attenzione ai segnali del mercato, ha evitato perdite. | By paying attention to market signals, he avoided losses. |

This vocabulary sheet provides a comprehensive overview of terms and expressions related to economics and finance, along with examples that illustrate the use of the gerund in context. Understanding these terms will enhance your ability to discuss economic topics in Italian and apply the gerund effectively in your speech and writing.
