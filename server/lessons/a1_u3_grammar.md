**Unit 1: Present Indicative of Essere, Avere & Regular Verbs**
===========================================================

### Grammar Study Notes

#### Overview

The present indicative tense is used to describe actions or states that are true in the present moment. In this unit, we will focus on the present indicative forms of the auxiliary verbs "essere" (to be) and "avere" (to have), as well as regular verbs ending in -are, -ere, and -ire.

#### Present Indicative Forms of Essere

| Person | Singular | Plural |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1st | sono | siamo |
| 2nd | sei | siete |
| 3rd | è | sono |

*   The present indicative forms of "essere" are irregular and must be memorized.
*   Note that the 3rd person singular form is "è", not "sta".

#### Present Indicative Forms of Avere

| Person | Singular | Plural |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1st | ho | abbiamo |
| 2nd | hai | avete |
| 3rd | ha | hanno |

*   The present indicative forms of "avere" are irregular and must be memorized.
*   Note that the 3rd person singular form is "ha", not "sta".

#### Present Indicative Forms of Regular Verbs

Regular verbs ending in -are, -ere, or -ire follow a predictable pattern when forming their present indicative tense.

**Verbs Ending in -are**

| Person | Singular | Plural |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1st | -o (e.g. amo) | -iamo (e.g. amiamo) |
| 2nd | -i (e.g. ami) | -ite (e.g. amate) |
| 3rd | -a (e.g. ama) | -ano (e.g. amano) |

**Verbs Ending in -ere**

| Person | Singular | Plural |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1st | -o (e.g. curo) | -iamo (e.g. curiamo) |
| 2nd | -i (e.g. curi) | -ite (e.g. curate) |
| 3rd | -e (e.g. cure) | -ono (e.g. curano) |

**Verbs Ending in -ire**

| Person | Singular | Plural |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1st | -o (e.g. parto) | -iamo (e.g. partiamo) |
| 2nd | -i (e.g. parti) | -ite (e.g. partite) |
| 3rd | -e (e.g. parte) | -ono (e.g. partano) |

*   The present indicative forms of regular verbs follow a predictable pattern based on the ending.
*   Verbs ending in -ire often have an irregular 1st person singular form.

#### Conjugation Tables

Here are conjugation tables for some common regular and irregular verbs:

| Verb | Present Indicative |
| --- | --- |
| essere (to be) | sono, sei, è, siamo, siete, sono |
| avere (to have) | ho, hai, ha, abbiamo, avete, hanno |
| amare (to love) | amo, ami, ama, amiamo, amate, amano |
| curare (to care for) | curo, curi, cure, curiamo, curate, curano |
| partire (to leave) | parto, parti, parte, partiamo, partite, partano |

#### Example Sentences

*   Io sono studente. (I am a student.)
*   Lei ha un cane. (She has a dog.)
*   Lui ama la musica. (He loves music.)
*   Noi andiamo al cinema. (We are going to the movies.)
*   Voi siete italiani? (Are you Italian?)
*   Essere e avere sono verbi ausiliari. (To be and to have are auxiliary verbs.)
